Established in 1996 by owner Justin Cupid, Best Choice Home Improvements is based in Essex County, NJ. Justin has been in all aspects of construction for much of his life and is committed to many more years of service in this field. After many years of setting a base of quality work Best Choice Home Improvements has earned the respect of it's clients and partners alike.

Best Choice is dedicated to providing service built on a foundation of strong commitment, high standards, friendly service, fair pricing, building great relationships, ethics, honesty and fine craftsmanship.

 

Best Choice offers a full range of remodeling from Bathrooms, Kitchens, Basements, Additions, Whole House Renovations, Restoration work, Conservatories and The Endless Pool. With hands on supervision done by Justin himself, customers are assured professional, efficient completion of their jobs.
